Russian & Turkish Baths 95C 10C Quintessential New York sauna. Old and grimey adds a bit to the charm. The cold plunge is over-chlorinated to the point of being unusable.
Paradise Sauna 90C 15C Saunachelin award winner for 2022. Idyllic interior. One of the top recommendations for Tokyo saunas. Female days on the 10th, 20th, 30th of each month. Male only other days.
BATHHOUSE 85C 15C True to overproductionized Williamsburg. Hilarious Aufguss experiences served daily represents the commoditization of wellness culture. Owned and run by a known Brooklyn grifter, so patronize at your discretion. Reasonably functional sauna and cold plunge.
Löyly Helsinki 90C 15C Helsinki's premier sauna experience.
Bota Bota, spa-sur-l'eau 80C 15C On an immobile boat.
ROOFTOP Sauna 90C 12C Bathtub cold plunges on a roof, of varying temperature. Experience sunset totonou at this one-of-a-kind sauna. Coworking space on the floor below.
Archimedes Banya 90C 15C The San Francisco option.
MONSTER WORK & SAUNA 90C 15C By the ROOFTOP Sauna team. Largest sauna room in Tokyo. Multiple seat tiers. A large cold plunge that cools to 10C on one end and 20C on another yielding a temperature gradient.
SKY SPA Yokohama 90C 15C Saunachelin winner. Skyline view. Multiple sauna rooms. Dedicated co-ed sauna on the upper floor with adjacent ice rooms. Hosts many aufguss championships.
Wellbe Sakae 90C 15C Saunachelin winner. Many distinct sauna rooms. A nice cold plunge inside an ice room.
COCOFURO かが浴場 90C 15C Highly recommended, off-the-tourist path sento. Popular among locals. Renovated and modernized. Every 30 minutes, a light show and music accompany an auto-loyly. Setlists change daily, mostly J-pop.
Koganeyu 90C 15C Modernized and renovated sento, originally founded in 1932. Popular among locals. Famous for its beer bar.
8HOTEL CHIGASAKI 90C 15C Hip hotel with a Saunachelin-winning co-ed sauna. At evening, the hotel pool is set to cold for a plunge you can dive into.
８HOTEL SHONAN FUJISAWA 90C 15C Hotel with a photogenic co-ed spa called 8 WATER CAVE.
King Spa & Sauna 90C 20C You don't need a hotel in NYC, just take the bus here and spend the night.
Personal Sauna KUU 90C 15C A perfected personal sauna.
SHIBUYA SAUNAS 85C 15C Five distinct sauna rooms; variations on the sauna theme. Two cold plunges. Half indoor, half outdoor. A lot of thought went into the design here. Saunachelin winner. One of the top recommendations in Tokyo.
Kinnokame 90C 15C A.k.a. Kinnokame, a delightful turtle and ginger-themed sauna in Akasaka. Cold plunge is infused with ginger. Loyly is infused with ginger essential oils. Free ginger sugar tablets and ginger water.
AIRE Ancient Baths New York 80C 10C Pretend you're in ancient Rome. Overpriced, but two cold plunge levels make it worth the visit.
SoJo Spa Club 70C 20 Instagrammable spot that people might enjoy visiting on occasion, but too posh for many.
Wall Street Bath & Spa 88 90C 15C A Russian bathhouse in the Financial District. Arguably better than the Russian & Turkish Baths, if not only for the healther chlorine levels in the deep plunge.
